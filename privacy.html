Privacy Policy for Dark Realm Puzzle

Effective date: April 22, 2026

Dark Realm Puzzle is designed as a fully offline single-player game. The app does not require account registration, does not provide online chat or social features, and does not transmit gameplay data to a remote server.

Information We Collect

Dark Realm Puzzle does not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, precise location, or payment details.

Gameplay Data Stored on Device

The app stores local progress data on your device, including unlocked levels, earned stars, and audio preference settings. This data is used only to support gameplay and is not shared externally.

Third-Party Services

Dark Realm Puzzle does not require third-party login services, analytics services, or advertising SDKs for its core gameplay experience in the current release.

Children’s Privacy

The game does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the app works offline and does not include account creation, it is designed to minimize data collection risk.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated in the future if the app adds new functionality or services. If that happens, an updated version will be published at this page.
